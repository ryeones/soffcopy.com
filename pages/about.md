i'm [ryan](https://www.ryeones.com/). i'm in my twenties, figuring it out like everyone else.

i grew up in an average household. no connections, no inheritance, no roadmap. just a restlessness i couldn't shake. going through the motions never felt like enough.

i studied [architecture](https://cde.nus.edu.sg/arch/) in university. [played floorball competitively most of my life](https://www.ryeones.com/press). ended up in marketing for [a travel startup](https://www.seeksophie.com/about). none of it was linear and that's kind of the point.

soffcopy started from a question i couldn't stop asking: what am i actually supposed to be doing with my life? i kept seeing people who seemed to have it together, living with purpose and direction and wondering why i felt like i was dragging my feet in the dark.

so i started building the thing i wished had existed earlier. not a highlight reel but a blueprint -stories worth sitting with, spaces worth showing up to, and doors worth opening for people who are still finding their footing.

my goal was always to get a 9–5 to fund the 5–9, to eventually not need the 9–5.

soffcopy is the 5–9. this is what it looks like while it's being built.

if anything here resonates, i have an open invite — [ryan@ryeones.com](mailto:ryan@ryeones.com)
